      How to Play

      • Each turn you draw a tile. Tap a highlighted spot on the board to place it.
      • All touching edges must show the same flower colour.
      • Tiles with no valid placement are automatically discarded.
      • When four tiles form a 2×2 square, a blossom token blooms at their corner — if you still have a matching flower token, it is claimed.
      • Claim all 8 tokens to win! The fewer cards remain in the deck, the higher your score.
      • The game ends when the deck runs out.
